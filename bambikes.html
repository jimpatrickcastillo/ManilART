Experience Ecotours with Bambikes

It's as if cycling isn't enough of an environmentally sustainable task – enter Bambikes:

Bambike Ecotours provides guided tours to guests while riding a bike. These bikes are fundamentally made of Bamboo and are hand-crafted by the villagers of Gawad Kalinga in the Philippines.

A brainchild of Bryan Benitez McClelland, Bambikes aims to “develop a greener community program” and “generate sustainable jobs for the rural poor”.

Bambike Ecotours currently operates in three main locations: Intramuros, Bonifacio Global City and Makati. Online bookings are available in their official website.
